OUR STORY

Our team comes with the experience and knowledge

We are a team of highly skilled individuals trying to help students overcome their fears and achieve success in maths. Led by Riya Jain, who is a mathematics teacher with a passion for education and implementing successful teaching practices.

She has experience working with students from diverse backgrounds and aims to further the cause of education.

We believe that every student has the potential to excel in maths, and our goal is to provide the support and resources needed to make that happen. With our engaging and interactive online tools and resources, students can learn at their own pace and gain a deeper understanding of maths concepts.

We are committed to helping students overcome their fears and achieve success in maths, no matter where they are starting from.

Riya Jain completed her Master's in Mathematics from Amity University and holds a Bachelor's degree in Science (Mathematics) from University of Delhi. She achieved excellent academic performance throughout her education, consistently displaying her dedication and proficiency.

Her accomplishments, skills, research, and academic background showcase her dedication to education, proficiency in mathematics, and contribution to the field.
